## Roleplay in Silverymoon

Roleplay can occur in solos (by yourself), with other player characters, or with NPCs played by staff, and earn XP if you run a timer while you type with others.

For best results, **Communication**. Talk before, during, and after RP. Discuss your pace, scene goals and boundaries for the roleplay.

### Scene Slot Limit

Players have **2 scene slots per character** until their second character is approved. Then, you are given an additional **3rd 'flex scene' slot**. An individual character may only be in 2 scenes at once.

Shop NPCs and Solos (if posted all at once) do not use a scene slot.

### Reminders

- Wyrmworks, Shining Scroll and Corvid's Quill are **Closed, for NPC scene use only**
- No running 'official' NPCs, gods/demon lords/named lore individuals (like Lord Neverember), or roleplaying fighting dragons (aka roleplaying any fights with enemies/monsters your character could not easily kill) on your own outside quest tables
- Unnamed town NPCs can be roleplayed, for extended interaction with named NPCs please request an NPC in [ğŸ‘€looking-for-rp](https://discord.com/channels/866376531995918346/911867303283544094). Staff may post an open scene for NPCs there as well

## RPXP

RPXP accrued with the timer turns into real XP when you pay lifestyle in [â°roleplay-xp](https://discord.com/channels/866376531995918346/866544629214347284) at the beginning of every week. Timers can be run for both characters at once, but one character cannot earn double xp for being in 2 scenes at once.

**Solos do not earn RPXP.**

### Timer Commands

Run in [â°roleplay-xp](https://discord.com/channels/866376531995918346/866544629214347284), commands are pinned there also.

- **Start timer:** `!rp start`
- **End:** `!rp end`
- **End, asynch:** `!rp end asynch`
- **Pause:** `!rp pause`
- **Restart again:** `!rp start`
- **For fixing rp and info:** `!rp help`
- **Switch chars:** `!char name`

Amount earned is based on proficiency, and caps are visible at the bottom of timer.

### â²ï¸ Synchronous timers

Used when replying within a 2 hour window to their RP partner(s), simultaneous roleplaying. Begin the timer when you start typing, and end timers when your roleplay session has ended. Pause if needed.

### âŒ› Asynchronous timers

For replies beyond 2 hours between posts. When finished with one post use `!rp end asynch` to give an adjusted amount of RPXP, capped at 2 hours.

**Do not pause when finished, or you will go beyond cap and lose that XP.**

Two characters can spar with each other in rp channels without using attack commands, with both player and character consent. Hostility may occur, with consent; assume that a guard would stop any player on player crime or attacks.
